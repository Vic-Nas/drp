personal use and internal self-hosting, but prohibits operating a
publicly accessible hosted service for third parties.

You need a commercial license if you want to:

- Run a publicly accessible drp instance that accepts users outside
  your own organization

  a product or service to external users, whether free or paid
- Redistribute drp or a derivative work to others
