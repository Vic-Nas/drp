plans

drp is free to use anonymously. A free account gives you longer-lived drops and cross-device sync. Paid plans add storage, larger uploads, locked drops, and explicit expiry you control.

expiry in plain english

Anonymous clipboard drops expire 24 hours after the last view, or after 7 days total — whichever comes first. No way to extend them.

Free account clipboard drops expire 48 hours after the last view, or after 30 days total. Still automatic — you cannot set a specific date.

Paid drops let you choose an explicit expiry date at upload time: up to 1 year for Starter, up to 3 years for Pro. They can also be renewed, pushing the expiry forward by the same duration again.

File drops (anon and free) expire 90 days after upload regardless of access.

what "locked" means

On anonymous and free plans, after the 24-hour post-creation window anyone who knows your key can overwrite its content. This is by design for quick ephemeral sharing.

Paid drops are permanently locked to your account the moment you create them. Only you can edit, rename, delete, or renew them. No one can overwrite a key you own.

storage quota

Storage counts file drops only — text clipboards are free. Your current usage is shown on your account page. If a file upload would push you over quota it is rejected immediately at upload time with a clear error. Deleting a file drop frees the storage instantly.

password protection

Paid accounts can add a password to any drop they own. The password is stored as a secure hash (PBKDF2) — it is never saved in plain text. Browsers remember unlocked drops for the session. The CLI prompts for passwords automatically when needed.

free accounts

Signing up needs only an email and password — no credit card. You get longer expiry, bookmarked drops, export/import, and cross-device sync (drops are attached to your account, not just your browser cookie).

upgrading & billing

Paid plans bill monthly through Lemon Squeezy. Upgrade from your account page. Cancelling returns you to the free plan at the end of the billing period — your drops are not immediately deleted, but paid-only limits no longer apply to new uploads. Manage or cancel any time in the billing portal.
