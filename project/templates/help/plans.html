what "locked" means

Anonymous and free drops are unlocked — after the 24h creation window, anyone who knows the key can replace the content. This is intentional for quick sharing, but means your drop isn't private.

Paid drops are permanently locked to your account. Only you can edit, rename, delete, or renew them. Nobody else can overwrite a key you own.

storage quota

Storage applies to files only — text clipboards don't count toward it. Your usage is shown on your account page and in the dashboard on the home page. Dropping a file that would exceed your quota is rejected at upload time.

When a file drop is deleted (by you or by expiry), its storage is freed immediately.

free accounts

Creating a free account requires only an email and password. No credit card. Free accounts get longer expiry, saved drops, export/import, and cross-device sync (your drops are attached to your account, not just your browser).

upgrading & billing

Paid plans are billed monthly through Lemon Squeezy. You can upgrade from your account page. Downgrading or cancelling returns you to the free plan at the end of the billing period — your drops aren't immediately deleted, but the paid limits no longer apply to new drops.

To manage or cancel your subscription, visit the billing portal.
